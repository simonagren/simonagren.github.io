My Story

My happy place has been the golf course for the last 30 years. This is where I get centered, and time doesn't really matter.
My name is Simon "King of the North" Ågren, and I live in Northern Sweden. I'm a proud father of two boys that I get to have every other week.
I work as Cloud Security Success Manager (CSSM) for Onevinn. I drive strategic partnerships with both customers and Microsoft, strengthening our collaboration. As a trusted advisor, I work closely with customers, providing long-term strategic guidance and leveraging Onevinn's expertise to help them achieve their goals. I focus mostly on Micorosft 365 and Microsoft Purview.
I've been awarded as Microsoft MVP since 2019, which enables me to work closely with Microsoft. I track what's coming and try to predict business opportunities and how this maps against current and anticipated customers' needs.
I come from a developer background. Nowadays, I write less code and focus on helping companies get the most out of their Microsoft 365 and Security investment.
What you will see on this website is me writing about technical or non-technical topics revolving primarily:
  • Microsoft 365
  • Security
  • Power Platform
